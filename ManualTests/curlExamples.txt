 --- READ ME ---

During this project we have done lots of manual testing, this was primarily done in the frontend once that was working. 
When we got our frontend working at least 1 or 2 of us tested through different routes a user might follow to find 
any problems - we found this to be a very effective method.
Finally, before we got working front-end all manual testing was done with curl commands in the terminal, I have listed 
a few of these below as examples but many more can be found in the comments of pull requests and commits.
